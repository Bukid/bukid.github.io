About Me

This 2017 the major was held in Kiev, Ukraine and last year was in Boston both duely named the Kiev and Boston Major. The mechanics and aesthetics of DOTA has lined up grand tournaments and events boasting millions of dollars in cash pool prizes and beloved internet personalities from all around the making their mark and value in twitch streaming and youtube as content creators. As a web developer i look to contribute in the industry and push my potential to the max until the day i retire.
